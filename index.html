📋 Quick Tips

▸ Tap "Use My Current Location" to auto-capture GPS — works best outdoors
▸ Use the Quantity field to log bulk plantings in a single entry
▸ Native Indian species (Neem, Peepal, Banyan) are recommended for EVS projects
▸ Include soil type and weather notes for better long-term analysis
▸ Update health status anytime from the Growth Tracker tab
